# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards
Participants are expected to:
- Be respectful and professional
- Give constructive, actionable feedback
- Focus on technical merit and collaboration
- Assume good intent while holding high quality standards

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, intimidation, or bad-faith disruption
- Sharing private information without consent

## Scope
This applies to project spaces including issues, pull requests, discussions, and community channels.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject content and contributions that violate this code, and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation.

## Reporting
Report incidents privately to project maintainers via a security/reporting channel defined in `SECURITY.md`.
